My hobbies

Urban dance

Of the things I love most in the world is dancing. From the moment I learned about the existence of urban dance, I knew that was what I wanted to learn. The first class was an unforgettable experience since I was very shy and had never moved my body in a position other than the conventional ones. Over time I began to discover what my favorite styles are: House, Locking, Waacking and Hip Hop. Each of them produces different sensations in me: fun, happiness or serenity. Today I can easily say that this journey has changed my life. Dancing for me means freedom, passion, happiness and expression. There are times when I can manifest things with my dance that would be very complicated to do with my words.

Swimming

I started swimming since I was a baby. Once I entered school, my parents always took me in the afternoon to train. Swimming has taught me the essence of competing. In this sport you have two rivals: The clock and all those swimmers who are doing the same test. The tests that I have always liked have been those that cover many meters. My favorite is 1500m in freestyle followed by 800m and 400m (also in freestyle). I also like to compete in backstroke and if there was a longer test than the 200m one, I would surely do it. The best part is when one has already been swimming in full competition for approximately 18 minutes and no longer feels the legs. It is at that moment where you know that you must give your best and overcome fatigue to win.
