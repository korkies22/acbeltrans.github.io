Systems engineer:

Why and What

¿Why?

I am one of the people who believe a lot in technology. Technology has allowed the photo of a black hole to be seen for the first time or to discover the cure for smallpox, which alone in the 20th century was responsible for the death of between 300 and 500 million beings. In short, technology is crucial for the development of humanity. However, when I started this path that was not my motivation. I believe a lot in privacy, in freedom of expression and in transparency. Instead, I hate corruption, poverty and use our information without our consent. This is why I decided to study Systems Engineering: to help change that.


¿What?

So, ¿what do I do? I dedicate myself to the development of applications and software. I have knowledge in different languages and in different types of technologies or tools such as: SQDeveloper, AWS, MongoDB, Django, Bootstrap and Angular. My main interests are Artificial Intelligence (AI), computer security and the use of technology focused on medicine through synthetic biology.
